About Me

Hello

My name's Bao Ngoc Nguyen, but most people call me Jodie. Online, I go by CharlesKleeven.

I'm a full-stack developer with a background in computer science from MIT, working at the intersection of games, tools, and the web.

What I'm up to these days

These days, I'm building a narrative RPG and maintaining SoloDevelopment.org, a live community platform for indie game developers.

Both projects reflect what I love most: designing systems that eliminate friction, support creative workflows, and bring people together.

I also create illustrations and pixel art for my work, including the homepage background and the in-game visuals featured across this site.
